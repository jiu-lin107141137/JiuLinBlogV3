## Why this page exists

Images inside articles should feel easy to inspect without leaving the reading flow. This post keeps the content simple so the lightbox behavior is obvious: click or focus an image, then open it.

## Wide image

The cover image is intentionally large. In the article body it stays contained, and in the lightbox it gets more room.

![Purple night cover artwork used by the blog](cover.jpg)

## Smaller portrait image

Different aspect ratios should still feel stable. This avatar image gives the lightbox a more compact test case.

![JiuLin profile portrait](avatar.jpg)

## What to check

- Click either image to open the preview.
- Press Escape or click outside the image to close it.
- Use keyboard focus and press Enter or Space on an image.
